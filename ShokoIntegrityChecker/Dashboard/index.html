Integrity Checker

Re-hashes every file in your managed folders and flags any whose contents no longer match what Shoko has on record. Files with a changed hash are detached from their old release info and re-searched — the same outcome as rescanning a single file, just applied across your whole collection. Most come back unrecognized and need a manual re-match; some get picked back up automatically and need no action either way.
